TOP DESTINATIONS

These are the top 3 Tourist Destinations in The Philippines Starting from Luzon To Visayas and Mindanao

Hundred Islands National Park is the first Protected
national park in Alaminos, Pangasinan home to 124 Islands Scattered
in the Lingayen Gulf covering 16.76 square kilometers perfect
for boating and island hopping

Malapascua is a Philippine island situated in the Visayan
Sea, 6.8 kilometres (4.2 mi) across a shallow strait from
the northernmost tip of Cebu Island. Administratively, it is part
of the peninsular barangay of Logon, Daanbantayan, Cebu.

Surfing in Siargao is one of the best things you can do in
the Philippines. Find the best surf spots appropriate for
your level, where to join surfing camps, and what you can expect
from surfing travel packages.
